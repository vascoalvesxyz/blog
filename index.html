Books Are Mostly Useless

Despite the lack of reading that is to be expected of the digital age, we place books, information, knowledge, reason in general, on a high pedestal. On pure philosophical first principle, I believe you shouldn’t immediately turn to a book, to a prompt, to a search; choosing instead to always prioritize yourself, your own judgement, instincts and reason: refining them whenever possible. To be always searching for knowledge and yet to be completely hostile to it.
